The Dawn · Midi · Yofuke International Seminar presents a mix of colloquium and research talks on focused themes in arithmetic geometry. The intention is to both introduce classical topics to a wider audience, as well as report on recent progress. The Fall 2025 iteration, Explicit methods in homotopy and arithmetic, will feature two topics: homotopy Galois action in September, and Chabauty-Kim theory in October. Talks will be held over Zoom at 08:00 EDT on each date.

The DMY Seminar is organized by Benjamin Collas (RIMS, Kyoto University) and Christopher Rasmussen (Wesleyan University).
